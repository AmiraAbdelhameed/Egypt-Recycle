    üßπ How to Sort Waste at Home

    1. Rinse containers before recycling

      Keeps recyclables clean and prevents contamination

    2. Separate wet waste (like food) from dry waste

      Wet waste can ruin paper and cardboard

    3. Use labeled bins: Plastics, Paper, Glass, Metals

      Organization helps recycling workers

    4. Never mix food with recyclables!

      #1 reason materials get rejected at recycling centers

    5. Utilize local waste collectors in your area

      Many will take plastics, paper and metals for recycling

    üìç Practical Tips for Egypt

    In Egypt, you might not find recycling bins everywhere - but you can:

    Give bottles to informal waste collectors (they recycle plastic)

    Take electronics to phone shops or recycling kiosks (like at Carrefour)

    Begin with just one category - like separating plastic bottles
